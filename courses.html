Courses Offered

Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA)

A three-year undergraduate program focused on computer science, programming, web development, and database management.

Bachelor of Science (BSc)

A three-year degree offering specializations in Medical (Biology, Zoology, Chemistry) and Non-Medical (Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry).

Bachelor of Arts (BA)

A versatile three-year degree covering subjects like English, History, Political Science, and Sociology, leading to careers in humanities and social sciences.

Bachelor of Commerce (BCom)

A three-year undergraduate program emphasizing accounting, finance, business management, and economics for careers in commerce and banking.
